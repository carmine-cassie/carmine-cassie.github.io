Bronwyn & Skye

A character sheet for my Wanderhome character.

Bronwyn (she/they)

Playbook: The Firelight.

Animal: Raccoon.

2 I try to be:

  • Endlessly Patient.
  • A Good Judge Of Character.

2 I know I can't be:

  • Always Positive.
  • Everywhere At Once.

My look:

  • Mud-Soaked Cloak.
  • Wide-Brimmed Hat That Covers My Eyes.
  • Deck Of Playing Cards.
  • Book Of Small Rituals.

The light I still keep lit:

  • A rusted iron lantern that my mother carried before me.

The light that died out long ago :

  • Wisp, a small and forgotten many-faced god who was in love with my firefly.

What did I show Erin that she didn't want to see?
That her faith was at times beholden to convenience.

Why does my firefly like Elannia so much?
Because there's hope in her yet.

How did I save Erin when her inexperience tripped her up?
I taught her that she should keep her fallen star out of sunlight, so it doesn't fade.

What does Elannia say that I've gotten so tired of?
But why?

Some things I can always do:
  • Shrug.
  • Pet my firefly.
  • Illuminate all that is hard to see.
  • Keep walking.
  • Say: Watch your step.
  • Ask: Do you need a hand? They get a token if they accept my help.
During each seasonal holiday, choose 1 I haven't chosen before:
  • Teach my firefly something new she can always do.
  • Teach my firefly something else new she can always do.
  • Learn from my firefly an important life lesson, and get a new thing I can always do from any playbook.
  • Realize I don't have to be something I always forced myself to be.
  • Get a token whenever I put your trust in someone else's hands.
  • Spend a token to know how to get where I'm trying to go, no matter how far away.
  • I can always ask: Can you lead the way?
  • Take an unused playbook and add as much as I want from that playbook to mine.
  • I met someone new who needs my guidance more than my friends do, and I need to part ways. Pick up a new playbook and make a new character.

Skye (she/her)

I have a firefly, my companion that stays by my side no matter what. She sleeps during the day and lights up at night. She is luminescent. 2 to describe how we met.

  • The two of us grew up together. She is caring.
  • Fireflies have always helped my family. She is proper.
Some things she can always do:
  • Shed light on the shadows of the world.
  • Lead the way.
  • Protect someone else from the world.
  • Inconvenience herself to help someone else.
  • Judge something for its inappropriateness.
  • Struggle to get something new.

Luminescent

  • Shed light on the shadows of the world.
  • Lead the way.
  • Non-verbally ask: What is true about you that you keep from everyone? If they want to withhold an answer from her, they're going to need to spend a token.

Caring

  • Protect someone else from the world.
  • Inconvenience herself to help someone else.
  • Ask: What do you need right now?

Proper

  • Explain how things have been handled in the past.
  • Judge something for its inappropriateness.
  • Struggle to get something new.
