The Goals

Some of my goals have a bit more to them than fit in the little bingo board squares, so here I've got some room to give extra details about each!

A1: Ls 50 Hours

At the start of the year, I had, uh, 45 minutes on my Ls, so, wanna get a bit more progress than that. 50 hours seems a good number, that's like an hour every weekend!

B1: Makeup

I tried to have concrete definitions of success for a lot of these (hence all the numbers), but this one's softer. I just want to learn enough to be proud of myself :3

C1: Good CG Art Piece

Haven't done art in a while! This goal is to help me get back into it. "Good" is by my judgement - I'm striving for that feeling where the art gets me, like enthralled.

D1: Coding Project

A coding project is like an art project, but more employable! Can't be for work or uni, has to be a personal project.

E1: Grad Offer

I'm bad at having the motivation to apply for jobs, so by having this on my bingo board, when I look at the board to see how to spend my time, I'll get a little reminder to apply.

A2: Try Bookbinding

Specifically, like, handbinding a book. I think this would be cool for a notebook for a TTRPG character. I want to buy paper of the right grain and use a bone folder and stitch and glue signatures, I think it would be fun!

B2: 10 Theater Visits

Live music, live performance, it always blows me away, it's so amazing to experience. If I go to 10 shows I'll have had a very nice year :D

C2: Tattoo

I want to make a claim to my body.

D2: Learn a new Piano/Lyre Song

Every time I sit down at an instrument, I play, like, the one song I already know, because I find it frustrating to learn new songs. I want to push through.

E2: Do 3 Pinks

Pink is a difficulty in my bouldering gym, around V3. Usually I find I can flash most teals (V2ish), but have no idea how to start pinks - this goal is designed to help me persevere and overcome the difficulty spike between teal and pink.

A3: Go Off-Grid for a Weekend

I think it would be nice. Off-grid here means not using the internet - with potential exceptions for like, maps and train timetables.

B3: Playtest Own TTRPG

I love TTRPG design, I think the medium is awesome! I don't want to make this "write" a TTRPG or "finish" a TTRPG - I want to reach a point where it should be playtested and then actually playtest it, instead of leaving it to gather dust.

C3: Turn 21 [✓]

My free space! I think it's fun that my free space can increment each year.

D3: 60kg

Okay, so, at the start of the year I'm sitting around 50kg, which is, real underweight for my height (I'm tall :3). Gaining 10kgs in a year is a bit of a crazy goal, I've been told, so:

If I don't crack 54kg by July, I'll reduce the goal to 55kg.

My reasons for this goal aren't just, make number go up. What I actually want to do is more consistently eat 3 meals, have more energy, eat more snacks, and have more fat reserves, so I don't melt through the muscle I'm gaining when I excercise.

I wanted to hit all these factors while having a specific goal that fit in a bingo box, so, a round number target will have to do.

E3: 5 New Wardrobe Pieces

My wardrobe could do with just an itty bit of expanding, and also, going shopping with friends is fun!

A4: Road Trip w+ Frens

Okay so not a "Road Trip", per se, any kind of holiday adventure will do!

B4: Practice Voice

Bit of a broad goal, I just want to do something that involves recording my voice, listening to the recording, and then trying to improve. This could range from voice training to singing!

C4: Boulder x8 in 1 Month

Consistency is important! Maybe a bit of a crazy target, but I've got 12 attempts, so she'll be right.

D4: Watch 50 Movies

Last year I watched 44 movies, and this year, I wanna do a little bit more! Part of a larger trend I'm working on where I'm trying to be more intentional with my media consumption - less scrolling.

E4: 3 Wanderhome Sessions

Okay the actual goal is more complex - I want to play 3 TTRPG sessions, that are connected (not unrelated one-shots) in an indie system with good game design. I just picked "Wanderhome" because it meets those criteria and I want to play it.

A5: Put 3 Things on Website

I want to get my website off the ground, I think personal websites are awesome and I want one and it's within my power to make that a reality!

B5: 5 New Recipes

It's easy to follow a recipe, but having to look up and refer to a recipe is a big overhead cost, while cooking. If I learn some more, I'll just have them up my sleeve, raring and ready to go!

C5: Get + Wear New Swimwear [✓]

I haven't had swimwear I liked in like, 4 years, so I haven't gone swimming or to the beach. Seems a pretty easy problem to solve, and swimming is fun!

D5: 50 Auslan Signs

Ooh this is another one that didn't fit in the box. I've a friend that's considering getting into luka pona, so I'll also consider Conversational in luka pona as an alternate success condition!

Like, my use case for a signed language is communicating with my (hearing) friends when we're in a loud environment, or when one of them goes non-speaking.Auslan/luka pona both satisfy these use cases, so it makes sense to learn whatever my friends are learning.

E5: Finish Pact

Okay, this is a sneaky one. I don't just want to finish reading Pact, a webserial by wildbow. I also want to typeset it, design a cover, and get it printed, maybe even make a slipcase! I really like the idea of having physical items to represent digital media.
