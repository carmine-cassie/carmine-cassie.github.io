About Me

By a an unsurprising feat of selection bias, I think the most interesting things about me are the things I'm interested in. I'm sure they do reflect on me though, so I'll take that as good framing for this section.

My interests include:

  • Linguistics is good stuff. There's a pattern to things I'm interested in: computers, light, language. I like paying attention to some of the things we touch thousands of times a day and learning about the beautiful and underlooked systems that drive them. It's just so satisfying to know how vowels work, and linguistics fun facts are some of the best.
  • VFX, CGI, those art forms that happen via transparent matrix multiplications inside of the 'puter! I love the way my exposure to this stuff has made me think about optics and how light works - light is very gorgeous stuff.
  • The color red.
  • Computer Science: I did a whole dang degree on this! It's very fun to solve problems and learn things and make things. Unfortunately, as I'm leaving university, I'm finding that my entire prospective industry has been swallowed into overexcited AI speculation, which I'm not a huge fan of.
  • You know I love a trip to the art gallery. As part of the VFX degree I did, I had to delve a little bit into the wonderful world of fine arts academia, which helped me fall in love with essays. I know recognise that modern art kinda hits.
  • Been getting into musical theatre recently. My top three would be Hadestown, Ghost Quartet, and Cabaret. As a result, I sing a lot, which has been really lovely from a body positivity standpoint - I like my voice!
  • Typesetting and bookbinding is fun, sometimes I play with Typst or a bone folder!
